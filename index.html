Common questions
How does an engagement work?

We start with a diagnostic of your operation, then agree a focused scope — usually a mix of fractional COO time and hands-on implementation. Most engagements run monthly, with clear priorities each cycle.

What does it cost?

It's scoped to your business and the depth of support you need. After an initial conversation we'll put together a proposal that reflects your actual operation — no generic packages.

Are you full-time or fractional?

Fractional. You get senior operational leadership without the cost or commitment of a full-time executive — as much or as little as the business needs.

Do you work remotely or on-site?

Both. We're based in Western Australia and work with businesses on-site and remotely, depending on what the work calls for.

How quickly can we start?

Usually within a couple of weeks. The first step is a confidential conversation to understand where things stand and whether we're the right fit.
